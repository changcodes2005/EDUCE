About Us

We are a student run organization

Due to the lack of opportunities in Oman... etc write it.

EDUCE is a student-led organization founded in 2023 by Rui Chang Lyu, a junior (grade 11) student at ABA Oman International School. Our goal is to provide free coding lessons to people who are interested in learning how to code and help bridge the digital divide by providing access to computer science education for everyone.

Our organization is run by a team of dedicated students who are passionate about coding and want to share their knowledge with others. We offer a variety of coding lessons and assistance with coding projects, as well as a mentorship program for those who want to take their skills to the next level.

In addition to our regular coding lessons, we also plan to organize events and meetups to bring together people who are interested in coding and computer science. These events will provide a platform for people to connect, collaborate, and learn from each other.

What is the cost of the courses?

The cost of the courses is absolutely free! It is available for anyone who is interested.

What is the duration of the course?

Each course is 1 month long, with classes held twice a week for 45 minutes or 1 hour.

Do I need prior experience to join the course?

No prior experience is required to join the course. The courses are designed for beginners.

What are the course materials provided?

Course materials includes online resources, hands-on exercises, weekly help.

What do I need for the courses??

All you need is a working computer and access to the internet, everything is digital.
