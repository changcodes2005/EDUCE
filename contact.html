ABOUT US

EDUCE is a student-led organization founded by Rui Chang Lyu, with the goal of bridging the digital divide by providing free coding lessons for everyone.
The organization is run by a team of dedicated students and offers a variety of coding lessons and assistance with coding projects, as well as a mentorship program.
They also plan to organize events and meetups for people interested in coding and computer science to connect, collaborate, and learn from each other.
