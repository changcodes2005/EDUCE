Languages We Offer

Our lessons include 3 very popularly used languages, with 2 of them being programming languages and 1 of them being a markup+styling language. Have a look below at the languages we offer:

PYTHON

Python is a high-level, interpreted programming language known for its simple syntax, powerful libraries, and wide range of applications. It is often used in scientific computing, data analysis, artificial intelligence, and web development.

JAVA

Java is a popular, general-purpose programming language that is designed to be portable across different platforms. It is commonly used for building enterprise-level applications, developing mobile apps, and creating video games.

HTML/CSS

HTML (Hypertext Markup Language) and CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) are the building blocks of the web. HTML is used to create the structure and content of a website, while CSS is used to control the layout and design.

Our Services

At Add name org, we offer a variety of services to help people learn how to code and improve their coding skills. Our services include:

Free Coding Lessons

We provide a variety of coding lessons, including introductions to coding concepts and tutorials on specific programming languages. Our lessons are designed for beginners and are completely free to access.

Coding Assistance

We offer assistance with coding projects, helping people understand how to code and debug their projects. Our team of experienced coders are available to answer questions and provide guidance on your projects.

Mentorship Program

Our mentorship program connects people with experienced coders who can provide guidance and support as they learn to code. Our mentors are available to answer questions, review code, and provide feedback on your projects.

ABOUT US

EDUCE is a student-led organization founded by Rui Chang Lyu, with the goal of bridging the digital divide by providing free coding lessons for everyone.
The organization is run by a team of dedicated students and offers a variety of coding lessons and assistance with coding projects, as well as a mentorship program.
They also plan to organize events and meetups for people interested in coding and computer science to connect, collaborate, and learn from each other.
